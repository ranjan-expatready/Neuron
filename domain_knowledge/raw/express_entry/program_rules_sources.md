## Federal Skilled Worker (FSW)

- URL: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/eligibility/federal-skilled-workers.html
- TYPE: HTML PAGE
- EXTRACTED_NOTES:
  - Minimum requirements: skilled work experience (≥1 year continuous full time or equivalent part time) in same NOC/TEER category (historic NOC 0/A/B → TEER 0–3); must be within last 10 years.
  - Language: CLB 7 in each ability (reading, writing, speaking, listening).
  - Education: secondary or above; foreign education requires ECA with equivalency.
  - Proof of funds required unless exempt (e.g., valid job offer + work authorization in Canada).
  - FSW 67-point selection factor grid applies before CRS; factors: language, education, work, age, arranged employment, adaptability.

## Canadian Experience Class (CEC)

- URL: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/eligibility/canadian-experience-class.html
- TYPE: HTML PAGE
- EXTRACTED_NOTES:
  - Canadian work: ≥1 year skilled Canadian work in last 3 years; can combine part-time to full-time equivalent; must be authorized work; self-employment typically does not count.
  - TEER/skill matters for language: TEER 0/1 requires CLB 7; TEER 2/3 requires CLB 5.
  - No proof of funds typically required for CEC applicants already in Canada.

## Federal Skilled Trades (FST)

- URL: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/eligibility/federal-skilled-trades.html
- TYPE: HTML PAGE
- EXTRACTED_NOTES:
  - Work: ≥2 years full-time (or equivalent part-time) skilled trades experience in last 5 years.
  - Job offer/certification: valid job offer of at least 1 year OR certificate of qualification issued by a Canadian authority (province/territory/federal).
  - Language: minimum CLB 5 for speaking/listening and CLB 4 for reading/writing.
  - Proof of funds generally required unless exempt via job offer/work authorization.
